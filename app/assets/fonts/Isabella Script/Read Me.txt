Isabella Script
___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Isabella Script is handwritten stylish copperplate calligraphy fonts, combines from copperplate to contemporary typeface with a dancing baseline, 
classic and elegant touch. Can be used for various purposes.such as headings, signature, logos, wedding invitation, t-shirt, 
letterhead, signage, lable, news, posters, badges etc.

Isabella Script features 420+ glyphs and 169 alternate characters. including initial and terminal letters, alternates, ligatures and multiple language support.
